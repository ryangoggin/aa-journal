    • Learned the differences between BTs and BSTs and how all BSTs are BTs but not all BTs are BSTs

    • Graphs are just nodes with edges connecting them, don't need to be sorted or have a ceratin amount of parent/children nodes
    • Graphs can also have cycles, need to introduce the visited set to avoid infinite loops
